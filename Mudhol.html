About:


The Mudhol Hound, also known as the Caravan Hound, is a breed of sighthound from India. The Kennel Club of India and Indian National Kennel Club recognize the breed under different breed names. The KCI registers it as a Caravan Hound while the INKC uses the name Mudhol Hound.

Origin: India
Temperament: Graceful, Loyal, Courageous, Elegant

Weight: Male: 22–28 kg, Female: 22–28 kg

Colors: Black, White, Brindle, Fawn, Chocolate

Vaccination:


6-8 weeks : DA2PP*
10-12 weeks : DA2PP*
14-16 weeks : Rabbies**,DA2PP*
Annual Examination : Rabbies**,DA2PP*

Health issue:



Obesity is a serious health issue in Labradors because it can increase their risk of heart and liver disease, joint problems.

It's important to note that individual dogs may vary, and not all Mudhol Hounds will experience the same health issues. Responsible breeding practices, regular veterinary check-ups, a balanced diet, proper exercise, and preventive care are essential for maintaining the health and well-being of Mudhol Hounds.
